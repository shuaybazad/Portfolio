About

Design that earns attention in three seconds or less.

I specialise in large-format and printed media — banners, festoons, X-banners, and photocards — built for environments where you don't get a second look unless the first one lands. Bold composition, confident type, colour that survives the print.

I've worked with event organisers, retail brands, and artists who needed their message to carry across a room or a street. If your launch needs to be seen, let's talk.
